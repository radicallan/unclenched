How the resident journey works (MVP)

The first version focuses on a clean flow you can embed on your own site. No accounts for residents, no jargon – just enough intelligence to sort people into the right pathway and give them a simple plan.

Step 1 · Home & situation

Resident enters their address and a few key details: tenure, heating type, benefit status and main comfort/bill issues. Where available, we pull EPC data to understand the archetype.

Step 2 · Eligibility & route

A rules engine built around ECO4, Social Housing Decarbonisation Fund, Warm Homes Local Grant and local offers sorts the home into a likely grant or able-to-pay route.

Step 3 · Roadmap & next steps

The resident sees a simple three-phase upgrade roadmap with indicative impact and a clear next step – for example, booking an assessment, registering interest, or viewing trusted installers.

Who it’s for

The MVP is aimed at organisations who are already delivering or planning schemes and want a clearer way to engage residents and prioritise homes.

Local authorities

Climate and housing teams who need a resident-friendly entry point for ECO4/GBIS, SHDF and Warm Homes Local Grant activity – and a way to see where interest is building.

Social landlords

Housing associations and ALMOs who want to combine stock-level plans with resident-level signals about comfort, affordability and willingness to have works done.

Delivery partners

Retrofit partners who need better-qualified leads: homes that make technical sense and residents who understand the basics of what might happen next.
