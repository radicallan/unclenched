The ritual

This is where the brand becomes real: the sequence. Two minutes. Same order. No noise.

Morning — LUMEN
  1. Water first.
  2. LUMEN: take your day dose.
  3. One line intention (optional): “Clear, steady, deliberate.”
  4. Get on with it.
Keep the ritual small enough that you’ll still do it on messy mornings.
Evening — VEIL
  1. Dim the room slightly (if you can).
  2. VEIL: take your night dose.
  3. Two slow breaths. Exhale longer than you inhale.
  4. Phone down.
VEIL is an “end-of-day marker”. The goal is consistency, not perfection.

FAQ

The basics, in plain language.

What makes this different from generic “calm” supplements?

The system. Two formulas only, designed as a daily ritual — modern, restrained, and consistent. The brand is the routine, not a giant ingredients dump.

Is RITE “occult” or “clinical”?

Both in tone, grounded in execution. The occult element is the ritual and design language — not untestable claims.

What if a manufacturer can’t source an ingredient (like theanine)?

This page is built for swapping: keep the “function slots” (calm focus / decompression) and swap ingredients based on what’s available and compliant.

What formats can these be?

Capsules are the simplest launch. Later, you can expand into drops, strips, or cacao sachets — but keep the two-core system.
