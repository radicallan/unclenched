Why start with the journey, not just the measures?

Residents don’t think in kilowatts or PAS standards. Hearthflow gives them a clear story – and gives you the structured information you need to prioritise stock and partners.

Resident-friendly

No accounts, no jargon, no twenty-page forms. A small number of questions and a plain-English roadmap that feels realistic, not salesy.

Scheme-aware

Built around ECO4, SHDF, Warm Homes and local offers, so the routes it suggests make sense for how you actually deliver upgrades.

Actionable for teams

You see patterns: which homes are coming forward, which measures cluster together, and where partners can add capacity.

How Hearthflow works (MVP)

The MVP focuses on doing one thing well: turning one home story into a three-phase plan and a sensible route through schemes and funding.

Step 1 · Home & situation

The resident enters their address and a handful of key details: tenure, heating type, benefits and main comfort or bill concerns. Where available, EPC data helps identify the home type.

Step 2 · Route & stack

A rules engine maps the home into a likely route (scheme-backed, landlord-funded or able-to-pay) and builds a three-phase stack: fabric, systems, then generation.

Step 3 · Plan & next steps

The resident sees a clear plan and what to do next; you receive structured data you can pass to internal teams or delivery partners as qualified leads.
