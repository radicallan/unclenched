How Net Zero Navigator works

Under the hood it’s data and modelling. On the surface it’s a clear three-phase plan and a shortlist of funding options.

Step 1 · Baseline

We understand the building.

Address, EPC and open data tell us the building type and typical performance. A short intake captures usage patterns, comfort issues and constraints.

Step 2 · Modelling

We simulate realistic upgrade paths.

For that building archetype, we model measure “stacks” – fabric, systems and renewables – and estimate ranges for cost, bill savings and carbon impact.

Step 3 · Roadmap & funding

We design a three-phase roadmap with funding.

The result is a phased plan tailored to your site, plus a shortlist of grants, incentives and finance products that actually fit your profile.

Built for people who need a plan, not another policy PDF.

SMEs & workplaces

Independent shops, offices and warehouses that need to cut bills and emissions but don’t have an energy team or time to decode schemes.

Charities & community buildings

Community centres, village halls, faith spaces and CICs that need a realistic roadmap plus a clear view of what funding might support each step.

Landlords & small portfolios

Owners of a few properties who must hit EPC and net zero targets without overspending on the wrong measures in the wrong order.

What makes it different

Not just another grant list. Net Zero Navigator is an intelligence layer that sits between buildings, funding and delivery.

Data-driven baseline

We blend open property data, EPCs, archetype libraries and a focused intake form to understand the building before suggesting any measures.

AI-assisted funding engine

Policy and scheme documents are parsed into a structured rules engine. That means “you might be eligible for X” or “don’t waste time on Y – it doesn’t fit”.

Three-phase roadmaps, not one-off quotes

Most tools push a single product. We design a staged pathway over several years, so early actions support rather than block later ones.

Funding mix, not just “find a grant”

Roadmaps show where grants, incentives and finance make sense – and where self-funding is more realistic than chasing unlikely schemes.

Lead generation with standards

When buildings are ready, they can be matched to vetted delivery and finance partners – keeping quality and consumer trust at the centre.

White-label ready

Councils, utilities and membership bodies can offer Navigator as part of their own support offer – branded for them, powered by you.
