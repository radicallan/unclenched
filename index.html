The daily ritual

Two minutes. Same order. No chaos. You’re building a system, not a shelf.

Morning — LUMEN
  1. Cleanse gently (or rinse if you’re dry-prone).
  2. Apply LUMEN: one pump, full face.
  3. Moisturiser only if needed.
  4. Finish with SPF every day.
Tip: LUMEN should layer cleanly under SPF. Keep your AM routine boring on purpose.
Night — VEIL
  1. Cleanse (double-cleanse if you wore SPF/makeup).
  2. Apply VEIL: slow, even layer.
  3. Optional: moisturiser on top if you’re dry.
  4. Stop. Let the night do the work.
Tip: Consistency beats intensity. If you add actives later, keep VEIL as the comfort anchor.

FAQ

The basics, in plain language.

Is this “occult” or “clinical”?

Both in tone, clinical in practice. The ritual is the experience. The formulation philosophy stays grounded: clear purpose, tolerability, transparency.

Will LUMEN/VEIL be fragrance-free?

You can offer fragrance-free as the default and keep any scented variant subtle. Fragrance-free makes the brand feel more serious and inclusive.

How do I expand the line later without ruining it?

Keep a “two-core” rule. Add only supporting satellites: mist, patches, cleansing balm, or a single targeted active later—never ten serums.

Where do ingredients and claims go on this page?

Add an “Ingredients” section per product with a short, non-medical benefit line. Save deeper proof for a PDF or blog post linked from the page.
